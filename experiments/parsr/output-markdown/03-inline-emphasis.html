Inline Emphasis Examples

Strong Emphasis (Bold)

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. When you need to emphasize important terms or keywords in your text, the strong element is the semantic choice. It indicates content of great importance to the reader and is typically displayed in bold font weight.

Emphasized Text (Italic)

The restaurant received an excellent review in the local newspaper. Italicized text using the em element is used to emphasize content that should be read with stress or importance in a conversational tone, such as titles, technical terms, or foreign phrases.

Underlined Text

This document was last updated on February 21, 2026 and should be reviewed accordingly. The underline element can be used to highlight important references or to indicate dates and time-sensitive information that requires special attention from the reader.
