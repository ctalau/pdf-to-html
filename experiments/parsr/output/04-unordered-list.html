Shopping List

All-purpose flour Rice (brown or white) Canned tomatoes (diced, no added sugar) Honey Baking powder and baking soda Sea salt and black pepper Nuts and seeds (almonds, walnuts, sunflower seeds) Herbs and spices (oregano, basil, garlic powder, paprika)
