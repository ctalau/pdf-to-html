Chef's Tips

For the best results, use beef chuck which has the right amount of marbling for a tender, flavorful stew. Make this dish a day ahead and reheat—the flavors deepen overnight. You can also slow-cook this in a slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours instead of braising in the oven.
