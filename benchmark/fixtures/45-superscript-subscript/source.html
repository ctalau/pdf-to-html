Mathematical Expressions

The Pythagorean theorem states that a2 + b2 = c2, where c is the hypotenuse. Einstein's equation E = mc2 expresses the equivalence of mass and energy.

The area of a circle is A = πr2, and the volume of a sphere is V = (4/3)πr3. For compound interest: A = P(1 + r/n)nt.

The nth partial sum of a geometric series Sn = a(1 − rn)/(1 − r). Logarithm: log2(8) = 3.

Chemical Formulae

Water has the formula H2O. Carbon dioxide is CO2. Sulfuric acid is H2SO4, and glucose is C6H12O6. Ethanol is C2H5OH and ammonia is NH3.

Combustion of methane: CH4 + 2O2 → CO2 + 2H2O. Photosynthesis: 6CO2 + 6H2O → C6H12O6 + 6O2.
